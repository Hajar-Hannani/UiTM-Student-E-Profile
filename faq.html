Frequently Asked Questions About UiTM e-Profile

Frequently Asked Questions

The UiTM e-Profile system is a platform for UiTM students and staff to manage and showcase their profiles, including academic achievements, professional accomplishments, and personal details.

You can log in to the e-Profile system using your UiTM credentials (username and password). If you are a new user or have forgotten your password, please visit the Login Page for assistance.

The UiTM e-Profile system is accessible to UiTM students, staff, and alumni.
